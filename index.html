Upload Timeline Data

Upload your Google Maps Timeline export in JSON format. You can upload multiple files and data will be merged.

Drag & drop your JSON file here, or click to browse
Accepts: .json files · Multiple files supported

Processing...
  1. Open the Google Maps app on your phone
  2. Tap your profile picture → Your Timeline
  3. Tap the ⋮ menu → Settings and privacy
  4. Scroll to Location settings → Export Timeline data
  5. Choose a date range and tap Export
  6. The file will be sent to your email or saved to Google Drive
  7. Download the .json file and upload it here

Note: Timeline data may also be in Timeline.json or folder exports from Google Takeout.
